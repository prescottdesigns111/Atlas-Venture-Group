About Us

Built by fundraisers, for charities.

Atlas Venture Group was founded on a simple idea: nonprofits shouldn't have to choose between doing the work and funding the work.

Our Story

This industry has had a major impact on my life

Since starting in 2021, I've had the opportunity to meet and work with people from many different backgrounds, provide employment opportunities, and help team members build confidence, communication skills, leadership experience, and long-term career potential.

I'm also proud to have worked alongside many respected charitable organizations and to have helped generate millions of dollars in donations for important causes. Over the past five years, our campaigns have supported children, families, people living with health challenges, animals, communities, and environmental initiatives.

What makes this work meaningful to me is the impact on both sides: helping charities grow their support while also helping the people on our team grow personally and professionally. I'm proud of what we've built and excited to continue creating opportunities and making a positive difference.
