About Us

Built by fundraisers, for charities.

Atlas Venture Group was founded on a simple idea: nonprofits shouldn't have to choose between doing the work and funding the work.

Our Story

From one campaign to a national network

Atlas Venture Group started in 2014 when our founder, a former nonprofit development director, grew frustrated watching great causes struggle with fundraising tools built for corporations, not charities.

What began as a two-person consultancy helping a single food bank hit its year-end goal has grown into a team of over 60 fundraising strategists, campaign managers, and donor-relations specialists serving more than 320 charity partners across the country.

Today, we combine hands-on campaign expertise with modern giving technology — but our approach hasn't changed: understand the cause, build the plan, show up as a true partner.
