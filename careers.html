The Opportunity

Build a career, not just a paycheck

We promote from within — the vast majority of our office leadership started in an entry-level role. Here's the path.

1

Fundraising Representative

Start with full paid training — no experience required. Learn the fundamentals of face-to-face fundraising and donor engagement.

2

Team Lead

Start coaching new hires, sharpen your leadership skills, and take ownership of team performance.

3

Assistant Manager

Help run daily campaign operations, recruiting, and training — building the skills to manage an office.

4

Office Manager

Run your own campaigns, manage a full team, and own the results for your charity partners.

5

Run Your Own Office

Our top performers get the opportunity to launch and run their own fundraising office.

★

Your Timeline, Your Pace

Advancement is based on performance, not tenure — move up as fast as you're ready to.
